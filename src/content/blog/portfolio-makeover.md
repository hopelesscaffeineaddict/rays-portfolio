## Introduction 
About 2-3 weeks ago in mid March 2026, I had my first interview with Cloudflare (which I will now be interning at this summer!). Since I wanted to familiarise myself with Cloudflare's products and also had updating my  portfolio website being on the backburner since October 2025, I made it my goal to do two of those things at once: Remaking my portfolio to make it more representative of me, as a person, while using Cloudflare Workers to handle security/deployment

## So, what's the point of doing so?
As you've seen from the landing page of my website, this is my fourth attempt at making a website. My previous (3rd) portfolio used a really cool Astro Template (three cheers for Rei Ayanami!) since I have minimal frontend experience and I wanted to deploy something quickly, but it had minimal changes from the original, and it still felt slightly impersonal - like it was my website, but didn't really represent me as a person?

Also, I was slightly paranoid about my website being crawled or DDoS-ed, and I wanted an easy but also cost-effective way of implementing security protections (like WAF, DDOS protection)

I did want to remake my website, but I did not want to build a new one from scratch as it would take far too much time/effort, and I opted to build on what was already there and modify further 

With that in mind, that settles my choice of tech stack :D
